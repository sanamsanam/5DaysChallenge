How to make Pizza Dough

Forget frozen pizza dough! Make a homemade Pizza dough instead.This pizza dough is fresh and taste so delicious , that you will forget it is storeboug.

ingredients

  • White Flour: 4 Cups
  • Instant Yeast: 1 TBsp
  • Salt:1/2 tspas per taste
  • Baking Power: 1 & half Tsp
  • Butter: 60 gram
  • Egg 1 large
  • Water As per required

preparation

  1. Take a half cup warm water and mix instant yeast and wait for 5 mins, then add baking power and mix it well
  2. In a separate big bowl add flour ,salt ,egg and butter and lighty mix it
  3. Now add yeast mixture with flour mixture and start knead with warm water and mix it well
  4. Now start kneading a soft dough.you can use either hand or food mixer.Dough should be knead well without any lums and not too sticky(you can use dry flour but not so much
  5. Leave the mixture in a bowl and cover with a tea towel or clingfilm and put in a warm place
  6. After about 4 to 5 hours, dough will be airy and double in size.Now you can use with any sauce and topping and make a delicious pizza
  7. Enjoy!
